My Planner is a cute, themed to-do app for school, work, and daily life with a diary style feel. It lets you add, edit, organize, and track tasks by category, due date, and priority, with filters, sorting, drag-and-drop reordering, and a weekly overview. All data is saved locally and works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile.
